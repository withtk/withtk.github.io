How to play

The King Game is a fast-paced game that challenges you to think on your feet. The objective is to create a story following the rules of the game and keeping it engaging and entertaining. You can play with friends or strangers and vote on the best stories.

How to play

Create a story based on the prompt given

Use your creativity to keep the story interesting

You will have a limited time to write your story

Vote on the best story once everyone has submitted their responses
